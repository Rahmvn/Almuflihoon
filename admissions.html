FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

How do I know the right class for my child?

Placement is guided by age, readiness, and previous learning experience. The school can also speak with parents directly to recommend the most suitable starting point.

Can Tahfeez be combined with core academics?

Yes. Learners can combine Tahfeez with core academic learning so that memorization, Arabic development, and general studies progress together in a balanced way.

Is boarding available?

Yes. Boarding is available, and families can receive guidance on student care, daily routine, and the kind of support learners receive while in residence. Boarding is currently for male learners only.

Can parents visit before registration?

Yes. Parents are welcome to visit the school before registration to see the environment, ask questions, and understand the admissions process more clearly.

When are fee and material details shared?

Fee and material details are shared during the admissions conversation after the first enquiry, so families can understand the requirements before completing registration.
