FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

How do I know the right class for my child?

The school can guide placement based on age, readiness, and prior exposure.

Can Tahfeez be combined with core academics?

Yes. The school can present a broader learning structure for families who want both.

Is boarding available?

Yes. Parents can ask for guidance on the boarding option, routine, and student care during the admissions process.

Can parents visit before registration?

Yes. Visits, calls, and orientation conversations can be part of the process.

When are fee and material details shared?

Those details can be discussed during the admissions conversation after first enquiry.
