## 3. Website SEO

Keep the site technically clean and easy for Google to understand.

- Keep `robots.txt` live and reachable.
- Keep `sitemap.xml` live and updated when new pages are added.
- Keep pages indexable unless there is a reason to hide them.
- Keep canonical tags correct on every page.
- Keep descriptive page titles and meta descriptions.
- Keep strong `H1` headings on each important page.
- Keep internal links between related pages.
- Keep mobile layout clean and usable.
- Keep the site fast enough and image sizes reasonable.
- Keep structured data valid where possible.
- Make sure contact details are visible on the site.
- Keep the map and address easy to find.
