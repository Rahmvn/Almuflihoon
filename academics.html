Learning Pathways

Academic pathways and student support.

The academic structure combines Tahfeez, Arabic learning, and core support within a clear school routine for both day and boarding learners.

Ages 2 to 5

Early Years Foundation

Gentle introduction to memorization, routines, language growth, and classroom confidence.

Ages 5 to 10

Structured Tahfeez Stages

A defined progression in memorization, revision, and daily discipline.

Ages 7 to 15

Accelerated and Intensive

Focused support for learners who need a faster or more concentrated route.
