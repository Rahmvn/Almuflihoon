About the School

A school built on faith, discipline, and purposeful learning.

Al-Muflihoon International School is a faith-based nursery, primary, and secondary school in Lugbe, Abuja where Qur'anic guidance, strong character, and quality academics come together in a calm and well-ordered learning environment.

Mission, Vision, Values

The mission, vision, and values guiding our Islamic school in Lugbe, Abuja.

Mission

To nurture learners through Qur'an-centered education, Arabic growth, discipline, and supportive academics.

Vision

Founder's Message

A Message from the Proprietor

We welcome every family to Al-Muflihoon International School with gratitude, sincerity, and a strong sense of responsibility. Our vision is to raise children who are grounded in faith, refined in character, and strengthened by sound academic learning.

We believe education should shape the whole child. For that reason, our school combines Qur'anic studies, Arabic development, moral discipline, and supportive classroom teaching within a calm and well-guided environment. We want every learner to grow with confidence, humility, and a clear sense of purpose.

We also value the trust of parents and guardians. By the permission of Allah, we remain committed to working closely with families to provide care, direction, and consistent progress for every child entrusted to us. May this school continue to be a place of benefit, excellence, and righteous upbringing.

Founder, Al-Muflihoon International School
