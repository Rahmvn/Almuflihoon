Adult Classes

Adult Qur'an and Arabic classes in Abuja for steady, guided learning.

Al-Muflihoon International School also supports adults who want guided Qur'an memorization, recitation improvement, and Arabic learning within a respectful and encouraging environment.

Qur'an memorization

Adults can receive structured support for memorization goals at a manageable and steady pace.

Recitation improvement

Guidance helps learners strengthen pronunciation, fluency, and confidence in recitation.

Arabic studies

Arabic learning can run alongside Qur'anic study to support understanding and language growth.

Who This Helps

Adults who want a calm, respectful place to return to Qur'an and Arabic learning.

Many adults in Abuja are looking for Qur'an classes that feel supportive rather than rushed. These classes are designed for learners who want progress with guidance, consistency, and encouragement.

Ask about adult classes

Beginners or returning learners

Adults can begin from where they are and receive guidance suited to their current level.

Flexible learning goals

Some learners focus on memorization, some on recitation, and others on Arabic understanding.

Faith-centered environment

The setting values sincerity, patience, and meaningful growth instead of pressure.
